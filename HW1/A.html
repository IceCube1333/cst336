Pixel Art

A big form of digital art, pixel art is created by using
raster graphics software, where images are edited on the pixel level.
Graphics in most older computer and video games, graphing calculator games,
and many mobile phone games are in pixel art.

Sprite Graphics

A sprite is a 2D image or animation that is integrated into a larger scene.
It initially included just graphical objects handled separately from the memory bitmap of a
video display, but this now includes various manners of graphical overlays.
Sprites were originally a method of integrating unrelated bitmaps so that they appeared
to be part of the normal bitmap on a screen.
